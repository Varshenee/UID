About The Movie

"The Archer" is a 2017 American thriller film directed by Valerie Weiss. The movie stars Bailey Noble as Lauren Pierce, a high school archery champion who is sent to a corrupt juvenile detention center after defending herself against a violent attacker. In the facility, she uncovers a web of abuse and decides to fight back. With the help of fellow detainee Rebecca (Jeanine Mason), Lauren uses her archery skills to escape and seek justice. The film highlights themes of survival, resilience, and the fight against systemic corruption.
