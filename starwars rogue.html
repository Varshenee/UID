About The Movie

"Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker" (2019), directed by J.J. Abrams, is the final installment of the Skywalker saga. The film follows the Resistance's last stand against the First Order and the surprising return of Emperor Palpatine. Rey, portrayed by Daisy Ridley, confronts her heritage while striving to become a Jedi, and Kylo Ren (Adam Driver) battles his own dark side. The movie aims to conclude the epic narrative that began over 40 years ago. Despite mixed reviews, it was praised for its visuals and action, and grossed over $1 billion globally."
