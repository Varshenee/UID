About The Movie

"Ant-Man" (2015), directed by Peyton Reed, is a Marvel superhero film starring Paul Rudd as Scott Lang, a thief who becomes the titular hero. Using a suit that allows him to shrink in size while increasing in strength, Scott teams up with Dr. Hank Pym (Michael Douglas) and his daughter Hope (Evangeline Lilly) to prevent the villainous Darren Cross (Corey Stoll) from weaponizing similar technology. The film blends heist elements with humor and action, creating a unique entry in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. "Ant-Man" was well-received for its light-hearted tone and inventive visuals, grossing over $500 million worldwide.
