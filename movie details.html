About The Movie

Oppenheimer(2023) is a biographical drama directed by Christopher Nolan, centered on the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the theoretical physicist known as the "father of the atomic bomb." The film delves into his pivotal role in the Manhattan Project during World War II and the moral and ethical dilemmas he faced. Starring Cillian Murphy, the movie explores the profound impact of scientific innovation on human history and the personal struggles of one of the 20th century's most influential figures.
